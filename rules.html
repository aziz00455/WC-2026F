FIFA World Cup 2026: Tournament Format
1. Group Stage (June 11 – June 27, 2026)

The tournament begins with 48 teams divided into 12 groups of 4 teams. Each team plays 3 matches within its group in a round-robin format (Total 72 Matches).

2. Knockout Stage (June 28 – July 19, 2026)

After the group stage, 32 teams advance to the knockout round. This includes: The Top 2 teams from each group, plus the best third-place teams

From there, the tournament becomes single elimination — starting with the Round of 32 and continuing through to the final.

How This Game Works
Round 1. Group Stage Predictions

During the group stage, each player predicts the outcome of every match.

  • Pick one outcome: Team A Win, Tie, or Team B Win
  • Total predictions: 72
  • You can update your pick anytime until 1 hour before scheduled kickoff

Scoring: Players are ranked based on total points scored.

  • Correct prediction: 1 point
  • Incorrect prediction: 0 points
  • No prediction: -0.25 points (Penalty)
  • Maximum possible score: 72 points

Tiebreak: If players finish with the same score, their ranking order will be randomly determined for seeding purposes.



Round 2. Knockout Stage

Only the top 32 players advance to Round 2.

Each player is assigned one of the 32 teams that advance to the World Cup knockout stage. This assignment is based on:

  • Player ranking from Round 1
  • Official FIFA ranking of the qualified teams

In simple terms:

  • (Sorry..) You cannot choose your favorite team
  • Higher-ranked players are assigned stronger (higher-seeded) teams
